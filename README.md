# ClimaCloset with React + Vite

Are you tired of experiencing all different season of weather everyday? If so, then ClimaCloset is for you! ClimaCloset is a web-based application that is designed to help you pick the ideal outfit based on the current weather in your area. 
By accessing weather and climate data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)'s National Weather Service (NWS), ClimaCloset generates and suggests outfits from the user's own customized wardrobe which is based off of the generic types of clothing that he or she owns. Users have the ability to manange/view their wardrobe, update the clothing or accessory types they own, change their ideal weather preferences, and view the weather conditions in their local area.

## Features

- **Weather-Based Outfit Suggestions:** ClimaCloset recommends outfits based on the current climate data and your weather preferences to make sure you are always dressed appropiately.
- **Customized Closet:** Input and view your own personalized collection of clothes based on what types of clothes you already have. Each clothing item in the closet will have a corresponding icon to go with it. 
- **Add a New Clothing Type/Item:** Easily add another type of clothing and/or accessory to your virtual closet.
- **Weather View:** Access current weather information straight from the app to help plan your day. 

## How It Works
1. **Save and Store Account/Preferences**: By accessing Microsoft Azure, a user's created account and respective perfernces are saved so that the next time they login, they don't have to change anything. 
2. **Connect to NOAA Data**: ClimaCloset gains access to its weather data using NWS's API to get current information (temperature, precipitation, wind conditions, etc).
3. **Outfit Generation/Selection**: From the current weather that was viewed and the user's prerferences, the application will suggest the most appropiate outift from your closet.
4. **Wardrobe Management**: Users can add new clothing items or accessories which are categorized  by clothing type (shirt, pants/shorts, shoes, accessories), and view them all in their closet with their respective icons.
